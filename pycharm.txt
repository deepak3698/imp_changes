####some Shortcut's of the pycharm


1...ctrl+alt+i  (for indentation)

2...ctrl+/   (for comment  and also for uncomment )

*3...ctrl+mouse-wheel (for change-font-size(zoom) )

*for zooming you have to do some setting in your pycharm.
(File>Setting>Editor>General) then enable checkbox for zooming(changefontsize...)
